Introduction

Welcome to Mattnifico's Ready or Not Commander+ app! I created this app to improve upon Ready or Not's Commander mode, which I think is a good foundation but needs a lot more fleshing out.

Starting Gear

  • Primary
  • Secondary
  • Armor - None
  • Gear - Only weapon magazines

Contracts

***NOT CURRENTLY IN APP*** Before jumping into your first mission, check out the contracts page to get started securing better weapons and gear. One contract can be picked at a time, and will be obtained after a mission completion form has been submitted. Once the form has been submitted, the available contracts will update and the unlocked gear will be available for purchase in the armory.

Mission Completion Form

Once you complete a mission, fill out the mission completion form on the home page. In the textbox, simply input the letter grade received after your mission. Different grades give you different amounts of funding:

  • F: $0
  • E: $100
  • D: $200
  • C: $300
  • B: $400
  • A: $500
  • A+: $700
  • S: $1000

If the large map checkbox is checked, the amount of money gained from the mission is doubled. Personally, I consider the large maps to be Twisted Nerve, Greased Palms, Valley of the Dolls, Neon Tomb, and Relapse, but you can decide what maps you think are worth double funding.

Armory

Once a contract is completed, the unlocked gear will appear for sale in the armory. Currently, each purchase is a one-time unlock, allowing you to equip your weapons and gear on all soldiers in an unlimited amount. I'm working on a "hard mode" where all equipment must be purchased individually.

Inventory

The inventory page shows what weapons and gear you've unlocked so far. This is solely a reference page.

Evolving City

***NOT CURRENTLY IN APP*** Los Suenos is a turbulent and twisted place, constantly changing your SWAT team's situation. Every three missions submitted, a random event will appear, either helping or hurting your team. Some events include increasing or decreasing funding, changing the roster, or playing certain missions. A checkbox is also available to toggle this feature on or off.
